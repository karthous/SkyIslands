what's SkyIslands?



SkyIslands is a resort established by Jiyun Mei. It consists of two islands floating above The University of Queensland, one of the top universities in Australia. SkyIslslands mainly focuses on extreme sports such as skydiving, and it is designed for the young people who are keen on extreme sports.
