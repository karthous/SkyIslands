author information



This website is designed and inplemented by Jiyun Mei, a young man who is keen on information technologies. The logo of this website is designed on his own. He established his first website with his information technology teacher's help early in his junior high school and learned Visual Basic by himself. He also learned SQL when finishing his bachelor's degree in The University of Nottingham Ningbo China. When acting as the leader of the chess club, he learned to use various design tools such as Photoshop and Illustrator. Now he is a postgraduate student of The University of Queensland majoring in information technology and learning to create websites with HTML5 and CSS3 instead of outdated languages in the course of Introduction of Web Design.
